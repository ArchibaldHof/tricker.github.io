How The App Works?

Authorization

First of all, each user has to either sign-in to the system, or first sign-up, and sign-in after that. That provides us with all the necessary information about our users. And it also helps us to create the communities of people united by the common idea. Each new user can easily join us and start making the world better, and moreover cleaner place!

Take a photo

The main activity in our application is jogging and taking a photo. All you have to do is run and collect garbage, or make someone else collect it instead of you, or even better with you. Before and after clearing the area take a photo, mark the location and other users will be able to find you. Create a community of caring people and make the world cleaner!

Send

After the image has been taken, we send it on our servers in order to apply a Neural Network to it. The algorithm is based on a three stage Neural Network architecture, which first of all creates a so called "feature map" of the image. After that it specifies the regions on the image, in order to classify them on the last stage: if they are contaminated or not.

Post

After evaluating the region pollution status the server returns a picture to a client, and the new post is created. In general case, the system also provides a location on the map, so that other people coukd find this place and clear it together. After doing this they can take another photo, to ensure that everything is fine. All happens like this.
